Six Food Category

captivating term that unites six diverse food categories into a delicious symphony. From the wholesome richness of soybean delights like taufufa, to the creamy indulgence of soft serve, the sweet allure of cokai desserts, the savory crunch of snacks, and more, this name embodies a perfect balance of flavor, texture, and creativity. It's a celebration of culinary diversity, offering something delightful for every palate.

Soymilk Series

Fresh and creamy soymilk, packed with nutrients and a smooth, satisfying taste that's both wholesome and delicious.

Taufufa Series

Soft, silky tofu dessert with a delicate texture, offering a light, soothing sweetness in every bite.

Cakoi Series

A variety of delightful and rich desserts, offering a perfect balance of flavors, sweetness, and texture.

Snacks Series

Crunchy, savory, and tasty snacks made with high-quality ingredients, delivering satisfying and bold flavors.

Softserve series

Creamy, velvety soft-serve treats that provide a refreshing and decadent experience with every scoop.

Others Series

A mix of creative and unique food offerings, blending flavors and textures for an exciting culinary experience.
