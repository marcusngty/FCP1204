~~ About Us ~~

Our Story

IKO-soya was born from a passion for sharing authentic, high-quality, plant-based desserts and beverages that bring comfort and joy to every customer. The journey began in Kota Kinabalu, Sabah where our founder, inspired by the rich food culture and diverse flavors of Southeast Asia, sought to create a space where people could enjoy wholesome, delicious, and innovative treats.

The Beginning

Our first store opened in the heart of Sabah Kota Kinabalu, where we started by offering our signature Soy Milk Drinks and Taufufa (Soy Pudding), a classic dessert enjoyed by locals and visitors alike. With a focus on quality and flavor, we used only the best ingredients, including non-GMO soybeans, to create smooth and creamy drinks and desserts. These initial offerings quickly became a favorite among our customers, who appreciated the natural, plant-based goodness in every sip and spoonful.

Expanding the Menu

As our customer base grew, so did our vision. We wanted to offer even more options for our patrons to enjoy, which led us to expand our menu to include a variety of exciting and unique products. From there, we introduced our Ice Cream Series, featuring flavors such as Chocolate Lover and Classic Sundae, along with a wide range of Cokai (Fried Dough Sticks) with different savory fillings, and Pudding Series, including creative options like Perfect Two and Purple Lover.

The addition of snack items, like Fried Enoki Mushrooms and Plant-Based Nuggets, further enriched our menu, offering a variety of sweet and savory options that cater to all tastes. We also began offering a customizable dessert experience with our Create Your Own Taufufa, allowing customers to select their preferred toppings and syrups, making it a fun and personalized treat.

Our Vision

At IKO-soya, we believe in the power of food to connect people and create memories. Our goal has always been to create a welcoming environment where everyone can enjoy wholesome, plant-based treats made with love and care. As we continue to expand, we strive to stay true to our roots—offering unique, high-quality products that showcase the rich culinary traditions of Malaysia while embracing new flavors and innovations.
From our humble beginnings in Kuching, we are proud to be a part of the growing food scene in Malaysia and beyond, bringing joy to all who taste our creations.
