-- Cakoi Series --

Golden-fried and crispy on the outside, soft and fluffy on the inside, cakoi is the ultimate comfort snack. Whether enjoyed plain, dipped in soymilk, or paired with savory accompaniments, it's a timeless favorite.


Cakoi Plain

Crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, our Original Cokai is a timeless classic. Perfectly golden and lightly seasoned, it delivers a satisfying crunch followed by a chewy, airy texture. Enjoy it on its own, paired with soy milk, or as a complement to your favorite soups and congee. Simple yet flavorful, it's the ultimate comfort snack for any time of day.

RM 2.50

Cakoi Homemade Pandan Kaya

A delightful combination of tradition and flavor. Our golden, crispy Cokai is served with a generous spread of fragrant homemade pandan kaya, made from fresh pandan leaves and coconut milk for an authentic taste. The creamy, sweet pandan kaya perfectly complements the light and airy texture of the Cokai, creating a harmonious balance of flavors. A must-try for those who appreciate classic local delights with a touch of homemade goodness.

RM 5.90

Cakoi Cocoa Chocolate Sauce

A delightful twist on a classic favorite. Our crispy, golden Cokai is drizzled with rich cocoa chocolate sauce, adding a decadent layer of sweetness to the light, airy texture. The combination of the crispy fried dough and velvety chocolate sauce creates an indulgent treat perfect for satisfying your dessert cravings. Enjoy it as a snack or a unique dessert experience!

RM 5.90

Cakoi Mayo Chicken Floss

A savory fusion of flavors and textures. Our golden, crispy Cokai is generously topped with creamy mayonnaise and a layer of fluffy chicken floss, creating a perfect balance of richness and umami. The combination of the crispy fried dough, smooth mayo, and savory chicken floss makes this an irresistible snack or light meal option. Perfect for those who love a delicious twist on the traditional Cokai.

RM 6.90

Cakoi Cheesy Chicken Sausage

A savory snack with a flavorful twist. Our crispy, golden Cokai is paired with juicy chicken sausage and topped with a generous layer of melted cheese. The rich, gooey cheese complements the savory sausage, while the crispy texture of the Cokai adds the perfect crunch. Ideal for a quick bite or a satisfying treat, this combination is a cheesy delight that will leave you craving more!

RM 11.90

Cakoi Cheesy Tuna

A creamy and savory delight. Our crispy, golden Cokai is topped with a generous spread of flavorful tuna mixed with creamy mayonnaise and finished with a layer of melted cheese. The combination of the crispy Cokai, rich tuna filling, and gooey cheese creates a perfect harmony of textures and flavors. A delicious choice for tuna lovers looking for a unique and satisfying snack!

RM 12.90

Cakoi Shrimp Paste

A bold and savory twist on a classic snack. Our crispy, golden Cokai is infused with the rich, umami flavor of shrimp paste, offering a delightful balance of saltiness and aroma. Each bite delivers a satisfying crunch, followed by the deep, flavorful taste of this beloved Southeast Asian condiment. Perfect for those who enjoy robust flavors and unique culinary experiences.

RM 13.90
