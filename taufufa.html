-- Taufufa Series --

A traditional soybean pudding with a delicate, silky texture that melts in your mouth. Often paired with sweet syrups like ginger or brown sugar, it's a comforting and light dessert loved for its simplicity.


IKO Signature

A signature blend of beloved textures and flavors. The IKO Signature Soy Pudding features a smooth, creamy soy pudding base, topped with refreshing grass jelly, sweet red beans, and soft pumpkin tangyuan. The combination of silky pudding, chewy tangyuan, and the earthy sweetness of red beans creates a well-balanced and satisfying dessert. Perfect for those who appreciate traditional flavors with a unique twist, this signature treat offers a comforting and indulgent experience.

Grass jelly, Red Bean, Pumpkin Tangyuan RM 10.90

Perfect Two

A delightful combination of textures and flavors. Our Perfect Two Soy Pudding features the smooth, creamy base of soy pudding, complemented by the refreshing, slippery grass jelly and the sweet, chewy peach gum. This unique blend of ingredients creates a refreshing, indulgent dessert that’s both nourishing and satisfying. Perfect for those looking for a dessert that offers a balance of smoothness, crunch, and a touch of natural sweetness.

Grass jelly, Peach GumRM 9.50

Purple Lover

A vibrant and indulgent dessert for sweet potato lovers. Purple Lover Soy Pudding combines the creamy, silky texture of soy pudding with soft, chewy sweet potato balls and a rich purple potato paste. The natural sweetness of the sweet potato complements the smoothness of the pudding, while the purple potato paste adds depth and a beautiful pop of color. This unique combination creates a comforting and satisfying treat that’s both visually stunning and delicious.

Sweet Potato Balls, Purple Potato PasteRM 9.50

The Three Musketeers

A deliciously harmonious trio of textures and flavors. The Three Musketeers Soy Pudding features a creamy soy pudding base, complemented by crunchy crushed peanuts, chewy sweet potato balls, and sweet red beans. Each ingredient brings its own unique flavor and texture, creating a well-balanced and indulgent dessert experience. The combination of crunch, chewiness, and smoothness makes this a truly satisfying treat for those who love a variety of flavors in every bite.

Crushed Peanut, Sweet Potato Bails, Red BeanRM 11.50
