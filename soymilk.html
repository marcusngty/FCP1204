-- Soymilk Series --

Rich, creamy, and nutrient-packed, soymilk is a versatile drink crafted from the finest soybeans. It's perfect on its own or as a base for creative beverages, offering a deliciously smooth taste with every sip.

Hot Classic Soymilk

A warm and comforting beverage made from premium, non-GMO soybeans, offering a smooth, creamy texture and a naturally nutty flavor. Choose your preferred sweetness with a selection of syrup options: White Sugar, Gula Melaka, Ginger Sugar, or Chrysanthemum Sugar. Each syrup adds a unique touch, from the classic sweetness of white sugar to the rich, caramel notes of gula melaka or the warming zing of ginger sugar. Perfect for a soothing, customized drink to enjoy on a chilly day.

RM 5.50

Cold Bottled Soymilk

A refreshing, chilled drink made from high-quality, non-GMO soybeans, offering a smooth and creamy texture with a naturally nutty flavor. You can personalize your sweetness with your choice of syrup: White Sugar, Gula Melaka, Ginger Sugar, or Chrysanthemum Sugar. Whether you prefer the classic sweetness, the rich complexity of gula melaka, or the herbal notes of ginger and chrysanthemum, each syrup provides a refreshing, satisfying option for any time of the day.

RM 6.50

Grass Jelly Soymilk

Glass jelly adds a delightful contrast to the rich, mild flavor of the soy milk, providing a refreshing, slightly firm texture that complements the smoothness of the drink. This beverage is not only a satisfying treat but also a healthy option for those seeking a dairy-free, plant-based alternative to traditional milk-based drinks.

RM 7.90

Red Bean Soymilk

A delightful and nourishing blend of creamy soy milk and naturally sweet red beans. Our Red Bean Soy Milk offers a smooth, velvety texture with the added richness of soft, earthy red beans. The subtle sweetness of the beans perfectly complements the light, nutty flavor of the soy milk, creating a refreshing and satisfying drink. Perfect served cold or warm, it's a wholesome, comforting beverage that combines the best of both traditional and plant-based ingredients.

RM 7.90

Coconut Jelly Soymilk

A tropical, creamy treat that combines the richness of soy milk with the refreshing texture of coconut jelly. Our Coconut Jelly Milk features smooth, velvety soy milk, perfectly complemented by chewy coconut jelly pieces that add a fun, refreshing bite. The natural sweetness of coconut enhances the creamy soy milk, creating a satisfying, indulgent drink that’s both refreshing and nourishing. Enjoy it chilled for the ultimate tropical refreshment.

RM 7.90

Water Chestnut Popping Boba Soymilk

A fun and refreshing twist on traditional soy milk! Our Water Chestnuts Popping Boba Soy Milk combines smooth, creamy soy milk with crunchy water chestnut bits and chewy popping boba for an exciting texture experience. As you sip, the popping boba bursts with a fruity sweetness, perfectly complementing the light, nutty flavor of the soy milk and the crispness of the water chestnuts. A playful, delicious drink that adds a touch of excitement to your day!

RM 8.50

Purple Potato Soymilk

A rich and velvety drink that blends the natural sweetness of purple potatoes with creamy soy milk. Our Purple Potato Soy Milk offers a unique, earthy flavor with a smooth, indulgent texture. The vibrant purple color adds a visually appealing touch, while the sweetness of the purple potato perfectly complements the nutty undertones of the soy milk. This nutritious and delicious drink is a perfect choice for those seeking a comforting and wholesome beverage.

RM 8.50
