-- Snacks Series --

A wide array of flavorful nibbles, from savory to slightly sweet, designed to satisfy cravings anytime. From crispy chips to baked bites, these snacks are crafted with high-quality ingredients for maximum enjoyment.

Fried Inoki Mushroom

Crispy, light, and full of flavor, our Fried Enoki Mushrooms offer a unique snack experience. Each delicate mushroom is coated in a crispy batter and fried to golden perfection, preserving the enoki's tender texture while adding a satisfying crunch. The earthy umami of the mushrooms, combined with the crispy coating, makes this a deliciously addictive snack that's perfect for any occasion.

RM 8.90

Vege Popiah

A fresh and flavorful bite in every roll. Our Vege Popiah features a soft, thin wrapper filled with a savory mix of crunchy vegetables, herbs, and light seasoning. Each bite offers a perfect balance of textures—from the crispiness of the vegetables to the soft wrapper. Light yet satisfying, this vegetarian snack is a wholesome treat that's perfect for any time of day.

RM 8.90

Tofu Fries

C rispy on the outside, and tender on the inside, our Tofu Fries are a delicious twist on the classic snack. Each piece of tofu is coated in a light, golden batter and fried to perfection, creating a crispy exterior that complements the soft, flavorful tofu center. Served with a side of tangy dipping sauce, these fries offer a perfect balance of crunch and creaminess, making them an irresistible snack choice.

Fried Sweet Patato Balls

Golden, crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, our Fried Sweet Potato Balls are a sweet and savory delight. Made from mashed sweet potatoes, these bite-sized treats are perfectly fried to achieve a satisfying crunch, while maintaining a fluffy, tender interior. The natural sweetness of the sweet potato shines through, making these balls a delicious and wholesome snack that's perfect for any time of the day.

Plant Based Nuggets

A guilt-free and delicious alternative to traditional nuggets. Our Plant-Based Nuggets are made with plant-powered ingredients, offering the same crispy, golden crunch and savory taste you love, but with a healthier, meat-free twist. Perfectly seasoned and fried to perfection, these nuggets are a satisfying snack for vegans, vegetarians, or anyone looking for a tasty and wholesome treat. Dip them in your favorite sauce for the ultimate snack experience.
